ABOUT US

For over 16 years, TrendSetter Remodeling has been a family-owned, Dallas–Fort Worth based contractor trusted by homeowners who want clean, modern renovations without losing the character of their homes. We’ve been a steady part of the DFW construction community — known for quality work, honest timelines, and hands-on involvement on every job.

From custom home builds and high-end bathroom and kitchen renovations to lighting, finishes, and detail work, our team is on-site and involved from the first walkthrough to the final punch list. Clear timelines, transparent pricing, and communication at every step are non-negotiable for us.

As a family-owned business, we see every project as a long-term relationship.
